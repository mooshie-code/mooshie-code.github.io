Instagram Bio Character Limit FAQs

  • Do spaces count toward the Instagram bio limit?
    Yes. Spaces are included in the 150-character limit.
  • Do emojis count as characters?
    Yes. Emojis count toward the character limit and may count as more than one character depending on the emoji.
  • Do line breaks count?
    Yes. Line breaks and special characters are included in the character count.
